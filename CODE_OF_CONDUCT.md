# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

> **Work in progress.** This is a standard baseline for community interaction.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Positive examples:** welcoming language, respectful disagreement, constructive
feedback, focusing on community benefit.

**Unacceptable examples:** trolling, harassment, publishing private information
without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or reject contributions that violate this code.
